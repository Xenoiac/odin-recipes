Lasagna Recipe

Delicious Italian Dish

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casing removed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, or 1 tablespoon dried
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, or 1 tablespoon dried, divided
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 12 lasagna noodles (uncooked)
  • Olive oil for greasing

    Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage. Add onion and garlic, then stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, paste, basil, parsley, sugar, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, remaining parsley, egg, and Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers two more times, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  5. Cover and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, broiling at the end if desired for a golden top.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with parsley if desired.
